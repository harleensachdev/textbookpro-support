‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

What is GrindTheBooks?
GrindTheBooks is an iOS app that transforms traditional textbooks into structured, manageable study courses. It breaks down complex chapters into 5-10 day learning tracks with quizzes, progress tracking, and personalized study plans.
Which subjects are currently supported?
GrindTheBooks is currently optimized for AMC 10 (American Mathematics Competitions) and IGCSE (International General Certificate of Secondary Education) syllabuses. We're actively working on adding more subjects and curricula.
How do the mini-courses work?
Each mini-course is a structured 5-10 day study track that breaks down textbook chapters into digestible daily lessons. You'll get clear learning objectives, practice exercises, and progress tracking to keep you motivated.
Can I customize my study schedule?
Yes! GrindTheBooks creates personalized study plans based on your available time and learning goals. You can adjust the pace and intensity to fit your schedule.
Is GrindTheBooks free to use?
GrindTheBooks is completely free to use, with no in app purchases.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
You can contact our support team in the Support & Contact section. We actively review all feedback and regularly update the app based on user suggestions.
